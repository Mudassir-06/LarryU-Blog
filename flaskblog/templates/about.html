About Page

Blog @ LarryU is the place for Lawrentians to blog about different clubs and organizations. Don't know what being a part of a club you might be interested in joining entails? Want to get an inside scoop on the resources provided by a particular club? You've come to the right place! Blog @ LarryU is essentially a platform where you can find different kinds of blog posts - from ways to join a club to details about upcoming meetings and events, from resources provided by various organizations to articles about the student life at Lawrence - to get the most out of your extra-curricular life at college.
